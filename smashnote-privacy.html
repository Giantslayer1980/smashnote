SmashNote Privacy Policy Last updated: June 28, 2026 SmashNote is a tennis match tracking app. Version 1.0 is designed as a local-first app. It does not require an account, does not include advertising, and does not use third-party tracking. Data Collection SmashNote does not collect personal data from the app in version 1.0. Match records, opponents, venues, profile information, and preferences are stored locally on your device. Data Stored on Your Device The app may store the following information locally so that its features work: • Your player profile, such as nickname and tennis preferences. • Opponent profiles and venue records you create. • Match results, point records, match statistics, and share-image settings. • App preferences, such as language and onboarding state. Permissions • Location: Used only when you create or edit a tennis venue and choose to mark the venue location. • Photos: Used only when you choose to save a match or record share image to your photo library. • HealthKit: Used only for Apple Watch workout-related features, such as reading workout heart rate and active energy data and saving tennis workouts to the Health app. • Notifications: If enabled, notifications are used for app-related reminders or Apple Watch related experiences. They are not used for third-party advertising or tracking. Third Parties SmashNote 1.0 does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or tracking SDKs. Children's Privacy SmashNote is a general sports utility app and is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children.
